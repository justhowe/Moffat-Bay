Thank you for visiting our website. Here you will find all the information you need to help finalize your decision to choose us as your lodging destination.


Moffat Bay is a wonderful getaway site that offers something to do for all types of visitors. Come hike along one of the many trails for a lovely stroll. Enjoy some fine cuisine at our restaurant with a view that can't be beat. Not here for the outdoors? No problem! We also offer many fun indoor activities and events as well!

The real highlight of Moffat Bay though is renting a ship to go out onto the beautiful waters. Follow the Attractions Link on the left to learn more about everything we have to offer!
